Magic Brush


I recommend adding a clear resin finish to your Paiwt artwork. Here I am using my preferred clear resin, FAMOWOOD Crystal Clear Wood Finish. It works perfectly to protect your work and also brings out the colors.




After following the directions on preparing your resin, pour desired amount onto your finished work. Just like with painting with Paiwt, you will want to be working on a flat level surface. Use a spoon or lift each corner to make sure all has been covered and you have a level resin surface applied.


Paiwt with a clear resin finish looks and feels great! Thank you for visiting, add to your bookmarks (Ctrl+D) and keep in touch.
