Large Colorful Designs


This technique creates a large, colorful design. You will need: Paiwt Medium, 3 different Paiwt Colors, and Paiwt Expander.

Start by pouring Paiwt Medium, then drop a Paiwt color.


Next, drop your second Paiwt Color close to but not touching your first color.


Next, drop a third Paiwt Color close to but not touching the other colors.


Next, carefully drop multiple drops of the third Paiwt Color between your other colors, combining the three colors together.


Finish your colorful new design by using Paiwt Expander.


Excellent! You have made a new large colorful design! Thank you for visiting, add to your bookmarks (Ctrl+D) and keep in touch.
